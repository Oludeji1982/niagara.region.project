# ltc_supply_analytics
Operations Analytics
## Overview

This pull request introduces the **LTC Purchasing and Supply Analytics Dashboard** for Niagara Region Long Term Care Homes.

The dashboard provides operational analytics for food purchasing, menu planning, production tracking, and supply chain management across LTC homes.

The solution uses **Python, Pandas, and Streamlit** to transform procurement and kitchen production data into actionable insights.

---

## Key Features

### Procurement Analytics
- Total food spending across all homes
- Supplier spending analysis
- Product purchasing trends
- SKU count tracking
- Identification of duplicate or redundant SKUs

### Inventory Optimization
- Purchasing frequency monitoring
- Identification of potential panic buying
- Product turnover analysis

### Production & Waste Monitoring
- Prepared vs served meals
- Waste estimation
- Waste percentage tracking

### Menu Analytics
- Weekly menu demand analysis
- Diet and texture distribution
- Meal category insights

### Financial Monitoring
- Cost per resident metrics
- Budget variance tracking
- Food price inflation analysis

### Supply Chain Insights
- Supplier performance tracking
- Home-level purchasing patterns
- Product consolidation opportunities

---

## Objectives Supported

This dashboard supports Niagara Region’s food service objectives:

1. Reduce food waste and panic buying  
2. Optimize inventory levels and turnover  
3. Forecast menu requirements accurately  
4. Streamline purchasing and reduce SKU count  
5. Control costs while maintaining quality  
6. Track food overspending and leftovers  
7. Analyze inventory data across LTC homes  
8. Identify product usage trends  
9. Provide interactive dashboards for decision making  
10. Recommend supply chain improvements  
11. Support collaboration with procurement teams  
12. Promote sustainable and cost-effective purchasing  
13. Estimate food price inflation  
14. Identify purchasing behavior by LTC home  
15. Reduce redundant product varieties  
16. Categorize meals by service period  
17. Track budget variance per product  
18. Scale metrics per resident across homes
